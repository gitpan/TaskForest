Support

For support, please send an email to the TaskForest discussion list, "taskforest-discuss at lists dot sourceforge dot net," or visit this page to subscribe to this list. You do not need to be subscribed to this list to mail to it. If you're subscribed, your email goes to the list immediately, where I, or other TaskForest users can respond to it. If you're not subscribed, the email will be held until a moderator approves it (to prevent spam).

Please consider subscribing to this list and joining it. As the author, I have learned a lot from users, and your comments and questions help drive the development of the product, and help improve it.

If you need commercial support, please contact ActivMindz, Inc. at taskforest (at) activmindz (dot) com.
